QR Building Access

Scan QR codes at building entrances to instantly navigate, access building directories, and view schedules. Each building has a unique QR code.

Building QR Codes
How QR Access Works

1. Scan at Building Entrance

Every campus building has a unique QR code placed at its entrance. Point your phone camera or use the portal's scanner to read it.

2. Auto-Navigate in 3D

The smart campus portal automatically opens the 3D map, highlights your scanned building, and shows the internal floor directory.

3. Access Building Info

View room directories, faculty offices, lab schedules, timetables, and building-specific facilities and services.
